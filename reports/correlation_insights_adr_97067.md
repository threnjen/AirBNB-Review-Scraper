## 6. Recommendations for Hosts

1. **Invest in Luxury Amenities:** Adding or highlighting features like Jacuzzis/hot tubs, grills, firepits, and ocean or mountain views can significantly increase nightly rates.

2. **Increase Capacity and Bedrooms:** Consider expanding sleeping arrangements to accommodate larger groups (8-16 guests), as bigger properties command higher ADRs.

3. **Enhance Outdoor and Entertainment Spaces:** Incorporate game rooms, pool tables, and comfortable outdoor seating to appeal to premium guests.

4. **Use Descriptive, Experience-Focused Language:** Craft listings that emphasize spaciousness, luxury, unique features, and memorable experiences rather than just functional descriptions.

5. **Ensure High-Quality Kitchen and Laundry Amenities:** Features like dishwashers, washers, and dryers are more common in high-ADR properties and improve guest convenience.
